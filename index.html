About Us

The Iron Tribe provides premium bicycle rentals for tourists, students, fitness enthusiasts, adventure lovers and corporate groups. Whether you're planning a weekend ride, a team-building activity, a corporate wellness event or an outdoor adventure with friends, our fleet of professionally maintained bicycles ensures a safe and enjoyable experience. Our mission is to build a stronger cycling community while helping people explore, connect and stay active through memorable riding experiences.
